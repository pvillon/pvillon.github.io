I am a moving image archivist, amateur historian interested in film practices, and a library worker. Currently, I am an MLIS student focusing on Media Archival Studies at the University of California, Los Angeles, where I am a 2021-22 ALA Spectrum Scholar.

I am also a member of Black Hole Collective Film Lab in Oakland, CA and a co-programmer for Light Field(opens in a new tab), an international exhibition of recent and historical experimental moving image art on celluloid held in San Francisco.

I was born and raised between the South and East Bay of the San Francisco Bay Area, and I'm currently based in Oakland, CA.
